Matthew Wilson's Interview With Jeremiah Tatum

Let me tell you about what the objective of Base Camp Coding Academy is:

Base Camp Coding Academy is an 11-month program located in Water Valley, Mississippi. The program takes high school graduates and trains them, techincally and professionally, to become full-stack Software Developer.

The Interview:
